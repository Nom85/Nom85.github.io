Projects

A structured overview of my experience across marine expeditions, biotechnology, startups and applied ocean science systems.

Expeditions & Field Work

University of Gothenburg – Tjärnö Field Research

Designed and executed benthic biodiversity research in natural and artificial habitats, including species identification, sample processing and field coordination in marine environments.

Université Côte d'Azur – Lérins Islands Survey

Marine biodiversity monitoring using transects, species identification and ecological comparison with historical datasets to assess ecosystem change.

University of Valencia – Oceanographic Campaign

Multidisciplinary oceanography expedition integrating biological, chemical, physical and geological sampling, including Niskin bottle operations and sediment analysis.

Madeira Ocean Observatory – Field Operations

Marine field support including cetacean photo-ID sampling, algae cultivation, laboratory protocols and participation in over 20 boat-based research operations.

Biotechnology & Laboratory Systems

Solaga – Algae Biotech Systems

Research coordination in algae-based bioprocesses, experimental design, SOP development and support for LIVMAT living materials project.

Zilt – Algae Cultivation Systems

Design and management of experimental aquaculture systems with 26 tanks, including growth analysis, nutrient monitoring and SOP creation.

Bake Your Dog Happy – Marine Bioproducts Thesis

Master thesis on macroalgae-derived bioactive compounds using 19 extraction protocols and advanced biochemical assays.

ECOMARE / CRAM – Coral & Marine Systems

Laboratory research on coral systems, photobiology (fluorometry), water chemistry and controlled aquarium ecosystems.

IATS CSIC – Microalgae Research

Microscopic identification of microalgae species, genetic analysis (RFLP), cultivation and controlled experimental systems.

Bionexus Impact – Co-founder
